Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is RentalHub?

RentalHub is a website that connects renters with property owners and managers, making it easy to find and rent a home.

How do I search for rentals on RentalHub?

Simply enter your location and rental criteria in the search bar, and browse through our listings to find your perfect home.

How do I contact a property owner or manager?

Once you've found a rental you're interested in, click on the "Contact" button to send a message to the property owner or manager.

Renter Questions

How do I apply for a rental?

Once you've found a rental you're interested in, click on the "Apply" button to submit an application.

What is the rental application process?

Our rental application process typically includes a credit check, background check, and review of your rental history.

Owner/Manager Questions

How do I list my property on RentalHub?

Simply create an account and follow the prompts to list your property.

How do I manage my rental listings?

Once you've listed your property, you can manage your listings through your account dashboard.
